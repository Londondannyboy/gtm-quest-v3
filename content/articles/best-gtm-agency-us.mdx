## Evaluating GTM Agencies

### Key Questions to Ask

| Area | Questions |
|------|-----------|
| Experience | How many companies like mine have you helped? |
| Results | What specific outcomes have you achieved? |
| Methodology | What frameworks guide your approach? |
| Team | Who will work on my account? |
| Communication | How do you report progress? |

### Red Flags to Avoid

Watch for warning signs:

- Guaranteed results without understanding your business
- One-size-fits-all approach
- Lack of relevant case studies
- Unclear pricing or hidden fees
- Junior team executing senior-sold work

### Reference Check Process

Validate agencies through:

- Speaking with 3+ recent clients
- Asking about challenges, not just successes
- Understanding actual versus promised results
- Checking team continuity
- Verifying timeline accuracy
