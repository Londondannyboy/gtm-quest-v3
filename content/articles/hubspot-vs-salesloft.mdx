# HubSpot vs Salesloft: Complete Feature Comparison

HubSpot is an all-in-one CRM and marketing automation platform, while Salesloft is a specialized sales engagement platform. Understanding their differences helps you choose the right tool for your needs.

## HubSpot

### Overview

**Best For:** Companies wanting integrated CRM + marketing + sales

### Key Features

- CRM (free tier available)
- Marketing automation
- Sales automation
- Customer service tools
- Advanced reporting
- **Pricing:** Free to EUR 3,000+/month

### Strengths

- Integrated platform covering multiple functions
- Great for SMB and mid-market companies
- User-friendly interface
- Excellent reporting capabilities
- Free tier available to get started

### Weaknesses

- Not as specialized as pure sales engagement tools
- Less advanced AI capabilities
- Can become expensive at enterprise scale

## Salesloft

### Overview

**Best For:** Sales teams wanting specialized engagement tools

### Key Features

- Sales engagement workflows
- Cadence automation
- Dialer and messaging
- Team coaching
- CRM integration (works with HubSpot)
- **Pricing:** EUR 1,200-4,500/month

### Strengths

- Specialized for sales engagement excellence
- Excellent automation capabilities
- Easy to use for sales teams
- Works great alongside HubSpot

### Weaknesses

- Requires separate CRM solution
- No marketing automation included
- Less comprehensive than all-in-one platforms

## Key Differences

| Feature | HubSpot | Salesloft |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| CRM | Excellent | Requires integration |
| Sales Engagement | Good | Excellent |
| Marketing Automation | Excellent | Not included |
| Ease of Use | Very Good | Excellent |
| Price | Budget-friendly | Higher |
| Specialization | Generalist | Specialist |

## Which Should You Choose?

### Choose HubSpot If:

- You need an integrated CRM + marketing + sales platform
- You're starting from scratch and want one system
- Budget is a primary concern (free tier available)
- You value having everything in one place

### Choose Salesloft If:

- You want specialized sales engagement capabilities
- You already have a CRM (or plan to use HubSpot CRM)
- Your sales team needs advanced cadence automation
- You're comfortable integrating multiple tools

## Using Both Together

Many companies use HubSpot and Salesloft together. This combination gives you:

- **HubSpot:** CRM, marketing automation, reporting
- **Salesloft:** Advanced sales engagement and cadences

The integration between the two platforms is robust, making this a popular choice for B2B companies that want the best of both worlds.

## Recommendation

Choose HubSpot if you need an integrated CRM + marketing + sales platform. Choose Salesloft if you want specialized sales engagement and are comfortable integrating with HubSpot. Many companies use both together for optimal results.
