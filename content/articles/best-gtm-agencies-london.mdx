## How to Evaluate a GTM Agency London Partner

When selecting London GTM partners, assess these critical factors:

### 1. International Experience
Track record with European and global market entry. Ask for case studies from companies similar to yours.

### 2. Vertical Expertise
Industry depth in your sector (FinTech, SaaS, professional services). Generalists rarely deliver the same results.

### 3. Strategic Sophistication
Senior team engagement on positioning and messaging. Avoid agencies that delegate strategy to juniors.

### 4. Technology Proficiency
Marketing automation and martech capabilities. The best agencies are certified partners with major platforms.

### 5. Content Quality
Thought leadership and research capabilities. Review their own content to assess quality.

### 6. Regulatory Knowledge
GDPR and compliance expertise. Essential for European market operations.

### 7. Team Structure
Senior involvement vs. junior execution. Understand who will actually work on your account.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do London agencies compare to US alternatives?
London agencies excel at international expansion, regulatory compliance, and multi-market strategy. US agencies may offer deeper domestic expertise but less European market knowledge.

### Are London agencies suitable for US companies entering Europe?
Yes, London agencies specialize in helping North American companies navigate European market entry, including localization, compliance, and go-to-market strategy.

### What industries are London agencies strongest in?
Financial services, professional services, enterprise software, and SaaS dominate London agency expertise. Healthcare and industrial companies may find specialists in other markets.

### How important is in-person presence?
Strategic workshops and quarterly reviews benefit from in-person collaboration. Day-to-day execution works well remotely with established relationships.

### Do London agencies support non-English markets?
Most agencies have partnerships or in-house capabilities for French, German, Spanish, and other European language markets.

### What's the difference between a GTM agency and a marketing agency?
A **GTM agency** focuses specifically on go-to-market strategy and execution — product launches, market entry, pipeline generation. Traditional marketing agencies often focus on brand, creative, or single-channel execution without the strategic GTM lens.
