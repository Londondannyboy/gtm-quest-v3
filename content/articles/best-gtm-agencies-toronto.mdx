## Frequently Asked Questions

**When should I choose Toronto over US agencies?**
Toronto agencies excel when you need cross-border expertise, cost efficiency, or multilingual capabilities. US agencies may offer deeper domestic specialization in specific verticals.

**How long until I see measurable results?**
Expect initial pipeline impact within 3-4 months, revenue attribution by 6 months, and full optimization by 9-12 months for comprehensive engagements.

**What's the difference between a GTM agency and a marketing agency?**
GTM agencies focus on revenue outcomes across the entire go-to-market motion (positioning, sales, marketing, customer success). Marketing agencies typically focus on demand generation and brand.

**Should I hire full-service or specialists?**
Full-service agencies provide integration and efficiency; specialists offer channel depth. Many companies use a lead agency for strategy with specialists for specific tactics.

**How do you measure agency performance?**
Focus on pipeline generation, revenue attribution, CAC efficiency, and sales cycle velocity. Establish clear KPIs and attribution models during onboarding.
