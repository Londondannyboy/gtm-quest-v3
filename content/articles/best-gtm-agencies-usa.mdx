## Choosing a US GTM Agency

### Evaluation Criteria

| Factor | Questions to Ask |
|--------|------------------|
| Industry expertise | Do they understand your market and buyers? |
| Stage fit | Have they worked with companies at your stage? |
| Methodology | Is their approach aligned with modern B2B? |
| Team quality | Who will actually work on your account? |
| Measurement | How do they prove and attribute ROI? |

### Agency Models in the US

- **Full-service agencies**: End-to-end GTM support
- **Demand generation specialists**: Pipeline-focused programs
- **Fractional leadership**: CMO/VP-level strategic guidance
- **Channel specialists**: Deep expertise in specific channels
- **Technology-first**: MarTech implementation and optimization

### Budget Considerations

US agency pricing varies by engagement type:

| Service Level | Monthly Investment |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Fractional CMO/Advisory | $5,000-15,000/month |
| Tactical execution | $10,000-25,000/month |
| Full-service retainer | $25,000-50,000/month |
| Enterprise programs | $50,000+/month |

## Working with US Agencies

### Engagement Best Practices

- Define clear objectives and KPIs upfront
- Ensure executive sponsorship and alignment
- Provide access to data and stakeholders
- Commit to regular strategic reviews
- Build feedback loops for optimization

### Time Zone Considerations

For international companies:
- US East Coast (EST): 5 hours behind UK
- US West Coast (PST): 8 hours behind UK
- Consider overlap hours for meetings
- Asynchronous communication is common
