## Belgium B2B Market Overview

### Market Characteristics

Belgium's B2B landscape features:

- **Headquarters hub**: Many multinational European HQs based in Belgium
- **Three languages**: Dutch (Flanders), French (Wallonia), German
- **EU institutions**: Brussels hosts major European organizations
- **Strong industries**: Pharmaceuticals, logistics, financial services
- **Export orientation**: Small domestic market drives international focus

### Regional Differences

| Region | Language | Business Culture |
|--------|----------|-----------------|
| Flanders | Dutch | Northern European, direct |
| Wallonia | French | Latin influence, relationship-focused |
| Brussels | Both + English | International, diverse |

### Key Industries

Major B2B sectors in Belgium:

- Pharmaceuticals and life sciences

- Financial services
- Technology and software
- Manufacturing and chemicals
